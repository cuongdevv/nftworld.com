How to create an account?

Visit our homepage, click "Sign Up", fill in your details (full name, email, and password), agree to our terms, and verify your email through the confirmation link we'll send you.

How to verify my identity?

Go to Account Settings > Identity Verification. Upload clear photos of your valid ID (passport, driver's license) and a selfie. Verification typically takes 1-3 business days.

How to buy an NFT?

Browse collections or use search to find NFTs. Click on one you like, review details, and click "Buy Now". Ensure your crypto wallet is connected and has sufficient funds. After purchase, the NFT will appear in "My Collection".

How to sell my NFT?

Go to "My Collection", select the NFT you want to sell, click "Sell", set your price and currency. Review details and click "List for Sale" to make it available in the marketplace.

How to create an NFT?

Click "Single NFT ", add a name, description. Upload your NFT files (images), set royalties or attributes, then click "CREATE NFT" to create your NFT.

How to manage my collection?

Access "My Collections" to view, edit descriptions, update prices, or remove items. Use our analytics tools to track sales performance, views, and community engagement.

What if a transaction fails?

Check your internet connection and wallet balance. If issues persist, contact support with your transaction ID, time, and amount for quick assistance.

How to report issues?

Go to "Help" or "Contact Us", select "Report Issue". Provide details about the problem, including when it occurred and screenshots if possible. We'll respond within 24-48 hours.

Is my personal data secure?

Yes, we use advanced SSL/TLS encryption to protect your data. We never share your information without consent. You can manage your personal data anytime in Account Settings.
